GRAPHIC DESIGNER

Agile Solutions is on the hunt for a talented Graphic Designer who can create engaging and on-brand graphics for a variety of media. The ideal candidate will have a strong ability to translate requirements into design and have a clear sense of visual storytelling.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:

- Creating a wide range of graphics and layouts for product illustrations, company logos, and websites.

- Reviewing final layouts and suggesting improvements when necessary.

- Collaborating with the team to ensure consistency of designs across various media outlets.

- Preparing rough drafts and presenting ideas.

- Using graphic design software and working with a wide variety of media.

SKILLS:

- Proficiency in graphic design software such as Adobe Creative Suite.

- Strong creative and conceptual skills.

- Excellent attention to detail and ability to meet deadlines.

- Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.

- Strong communication and teamwork skills.

RELEVANT EXPERICENCE REQUIRED

- Proven experience as a Graphic Designer or similar role.

- Strong portfolio showcasing previous design work.

- Experience in both print and digital media.
